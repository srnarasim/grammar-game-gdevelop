King's quest to save the princess with sentences

This educational game teaches grammar through gameplay. Players control a king who must navigate through challenges and form grammatically correct sentences to save the princess from a dragon.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move the king (Left, Right, Up to jump)
  • Drag words from the word bank to the sentence area
  • Form a complete sentence with a subject, verb, and object
  • Example: "The King saves the princess"
  • Form the correct sentence to defeat the dragon and save the princess

Note: This is a preview of the game. To play the full game, you need to open the game.json file with GDevelop.
